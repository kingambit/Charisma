The objective of the CHARISMA project is to develop a non-proprietary, open-source software platform to analyze and visualize NDE and other infrastructure data and fuse sensor data with digital twin/BIM models. This open-source platform enables analysis and visualization codes and algorithms to be easily shared and integrated into industry research, and help foster the integration of asset inspection, management, and project delivery data for better asset management decision making and facilitating stakeholder collaboration and participation to advance NDT/E and BIM research.
